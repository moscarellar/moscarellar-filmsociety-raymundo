Why join The Film Society?


We have been showcasing films since 2005. Always as a sign of persistence and believing in the importance of people gathering towards the big screen. In pandemic times we still believe that gathering and living the experience of cinema is a human need. If you believe this is, please join us.

Screenings

Our biggest events are the screenings! Wednesday and Sundays you can join us. We have a wide selection of films, from classical to genre and cult/art house films, which normally are out of the mainstream cinemas. Please find what’s on here.

Film Festival

Our most ambitious event at the moment. Every year we showcase member’s work and transform our space into a museum where members are encouraged to display their artwork, films, installations.

Hands-on Workshops

Twice a year we offer a hands-on workshop focused on filmmaking. We showcase the final cuts at the end of the course. Interested? Please email us.
