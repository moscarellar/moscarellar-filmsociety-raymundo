Why join The Film Society?


Gathering and living the experience of cinema is a human need. In pandemic times be part of the resistance and join us.

Main Activities

Screenings

Our biggest events are the screenings! We have a wide selection of films, from classical to genre and cult/art house films, which normally are out of the mainstream cinemas.

Hands-on Workshops

Twice a year we offer a hands-on workshop focused on filmmaking. We showcase the final cuts at the end of the course. Interested? Please email us.
